Day 1: Arrival Hotel L'Abbaye

Hotel Check-in info

Managed by Alex Chen • Created Oct 14, 2024

Quick Logistics

  • Confirmation: #TRV-8829-XPL
  • Check-in: 3:00 PM (Early requested)
  • Address: 12 Rue de la Belle Chasse, 75007 Paris, France
  • Phone: +33 1 45 50 22 66

Upon arrival at Charles de Gaulle, take the RER B directly to Saint-Michel Notre-Dame. The walk to the hotel is about 15 minutes through the Saint-Germain-des-Prés district.

Notes for the front desk: Ask about the "hidden garden" breakfast option. They mentioned it in the welcome email but it needs a 24h notice. Also, confirm the airport shuttle price for the return leg.
